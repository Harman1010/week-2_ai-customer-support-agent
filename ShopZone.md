# ShopZone Frequently Asked Questions

## Q1. What are your business hours?

Our customer support team is available Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 8:00 PM IST.

## Q2. How can I contact customer support?

---

## Q3. How long does shipping take?

Standard Shipping: 3–5 business days.

Express Shipping: 1–2 business days.

---

## Q4. Can I cancel my order?

Yes. Orders can be cancelled before they are shipped.

---

## Q5. What is your return policy?

Products can be returned within 7 days of delivery if they are unused and in their original packaging.

---

## Q6. When will I receive my refund?

Refunds are processed after inspection.

- UPI: 2–3 days
- Card: 5–7 days

---

## Q7. Which payment methods do you accept?

We accept:

- UPI
- Debit Cards
- Credit Cards
- Net Banking
- Digital Wallets
- Cash on Delivery (selected locations)

---

## Q8. How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email.

---

## Q9. How do I track my order?

Go to My Orders → Track Order.

---

## Q10. What should I do if I receive a damaged product?

Report it within 48 hours with images. We'll verify and arrange a replacement or refund.

---

## Q11. Do you offer Cash on Delivery?

Yes, on selected products and eligible PIN codes.
